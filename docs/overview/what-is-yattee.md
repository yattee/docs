# What is Yattee

Yattee is a privacy-focused video streaming client for **iOS**, **macOS**, and **tvOS**. It lets you watch video content without tracking, ads, or account requirements.

## Key Features

### Player
- **MPV engine** -- PiP, AirPlay audio, background audio, Now Playing, auto best-stream selection
- **Storyboard thumbnails**, **chapter markers**, and **sponsored segment markers**
- **Subtitles** with multi-language support and appearance customization
- **Quality selector** with resolution, codec, bitrate, and size info
- **Pinch to zoom** and pan with snap options
- **Lock orientation** and **lock controls**

### Media Sources
- **[Yattee Server](https://github.com/yattee/yattee-server)** -- self-hosted backend powered by [yt-dlp](https://github.com/yt-dlp/yt-dlp), supporting YouTube and 1000+ other sites
- **[Invidious](https://invidious.io/)** -- YouTube frontend
- **[Piped](https://piped.video/)** -- YouTube frontend
- **[PeerTube](https://joinpeertube.org/)** -- decentralized video platform
- **Local folders**, **Samba shares**, and **WebDAV servers**
- **Network auto-discovery** for Samba and WebDAV
- **Optional auth** and self-signed certificate support

### Customization
- **Control presets** -- with export/import and iCloud sync
- **20+ configurable buttons** -- across 3 layout sections with glass and plain styles
- **Volume and brightness sliders** - configurable in player controls
- **Mini player** -- configurable tap action (PiP or expand), reorderable controls
- **Player pill** -- configurable buttons and comments pill mode
- **Gesture config** -- 3-zone taps with 9 actions, horizontal seek, panscan pinch
- **Swipe actions** -- 9 configurable actions on video lists
- **Customizable tab bar and sidebar** -- reorder, hide, set startup tab
- **Home screen** -- reorderable sections with card and list layouts
- **Themes and icons** -- 3 app icons, 9 accent colors

### Library and Sync
- **Bookmarks** -- with tags, notes, search, and multi-source support
- **Playlists** -- with YouTube/Invidious/Piped import and multi-source support
- **Continue Watching** -- with cross-device sync
- **Downloads** -- background, batch, queue management, resumable
- **Watch history** -- with configurable retention
- **Per-channel notifications** -- for new uploads
- **iCloud sync** -- across iOS, macOS and tvOS

### Community Enhancements
- **SponsorBlock** -- auto-skip with progress bar visualization
- **DeArrow** -- community-sourced titles and thumbnails
- **Return YouTube Dislike** -- restore dislike counts

### Multi-Device
- **Remote control** -- play, pause, seek, send videos, view playback status
- **Handoff** -- video, channel, playlist, search with playback position

### Privacy
- **No tracking, ads, or accounts required**
- **Incognito mode** -- disables history recording
- **Configurable retention** -- watch history and search history limits

### Open Links
- **Batch URL input** -- up to 20 URLs
- **Parallel extraction** via yt-dlp with Yattee Server
- **Clipboard URL detection**

### Advanced
- **Custom MPV options** management
- **In-app log viewer** with filtering and export
